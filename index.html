Yale Astronomy and Physics

Big observational data confronts big theoretical data:
We build interpretable machine-learning methods to pair modern inference with astrophysical insight.

Population III stars

From surviving primordial stars to heavy black-hole seeds.

Our Population III work spans the lowest-mass survivors still in the Milky Way today, the feedback of evolved and massive Pop III stars on their host galaxies, and supermassive quasi-stars that may seed the earliest supermassive black holes.

The Milky Way and its dwarfs

Measuring galactic history with stars and simulations.

We compare cosmological zoom-in simulations of Milky Way analogs and dwarf galaxies with inferences drawn from pulsating stars in the actual Milky Way.
