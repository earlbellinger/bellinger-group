AAS Nova featured Andy Santarelli's [work](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.17952) on quasi-stars and little red dots in "[Starring in the Early Universe: Black Hole Stars and Little Red Dots](https://aasnova.org/2026/03/25/starring-in-the-early-universe-black-hole-stars-and-little-red-dots/)", highlighting how late-stage quasi-stars may explain JWST observations of little red dots and the origins of early supermassive black holes. Congratulations Andy!
